Solving India's Urban Housing Deficit

Millions of families in India face housing shortages, rising costs, and long commutes. Our mission is to provide affordable, sustainable, and accessible urban housing for all, improving quality of life and building stronger communities.

The Problem

India's urban housing shortage has increased by 54% since 2012. Middle- and lower-income families are pushed further from city centers, increasing commute times and lowering quality of life. Our venture aims to address this gap with innovative, scalable solutions.
